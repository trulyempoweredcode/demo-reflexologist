Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 March 2026

Who We Are

Helen Drury Reflexology is the data controller responsible for your personal data. I am based at 31 The Green, Stratford-upon-Avon, CV37. If you have any questions about this policy, please contact me at email@yourdomain.com or on 07700 900 205.

What Data We Collect

I may collect and process the following data:

  • Contact form submissions: name, email address, phone number, and your message
  • Appointment bookings: name, contact details, and appointment preferences
  • Health information: details you share during consultations relevant to your reflexology treatment
  • Payment information: transaction records (I do not store card details)

How We Use Your Data

Your personal data is used to:

  • Respond to your enquiries
  • Provide reflexology treatments and maintain treatment records
  • Send appointment reminders
  • Comply with legal and professional obligations

Legal Basis for Processing

I process your data on the following legal bases:

  • Consent: when you submit a contact form or provide health information
  • Contract: to provide treatments you have booked and paid for
  • Legitimate interest: to manage my practice and respond to enquiries
  • Legal obligation: to maintain records as required by my professional body and HMRC

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely and access is limited to me alone. Digital records are password-protected and encrypted where possible. Paper records are kept in a locked cabinet. I take all reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure.

How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Contact enquiries: 12 months
  • Client treatment records: 8 years (in line with professional body guidance)
  • Payment records: 7 years (HMRC requirement)
  • Website analytics: 26 months

Data Sharing

I do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data may be shared with:

  • My website hosting provider (for contact form processing)
  • HMRC (where legally required)
  • My professional body (in the event of a complaint or audit)

Your Rights

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification — ask me to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — ask me to delete your data (where applicable)
  • Restriction — ask me to limit how I use your data
  • Portability — request your data in a transferable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, without affecting prior processing

Children's Privacy

I do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16 without parental consent. If a young person wishes to receive reflexology, a parent or guardian must provide consent and be present during consultations.

Complaints

If you are unhappy with how I have handled your data, you have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO):

  • Website: ico.org.uk
  • Helpline: 0303 123 1113
