Reflexology Services

Every treatment is tailored to your individual needs. Whether you're seeking relief from a specific condition or simply want to restore balance, there's a reflexology approach that's right for you.

Stress & Anxiety Relief

Stress is one of the most common reasons people come to me — and one of the areas where reflexology truly shines. By working on specific reflex points connected to the adrenal glands, nervous system, and brain, I help activate your body's parasympathetic response, shifting you out of "fight or flight" and into deep relaxation.

Many clients describe a profound sense of calm that lasts well beyond the session. Over a course of treatments, reflexology can help reset your baseline stress levels, improve your resilience, and give you back a sense of control.

  • General stress and overwhelm
  • Anxiety and panic
  • Burnout and emotional exhaustion
  • Tension headaches
  • Difficulty switching off

Pain & Tension Management

Reflexology can offer real relief for chronic and acute pain. By stimulating the reflex points related to the spine, joints, and musculoskeletal system, we encourage the body to release endorphins, improve circulation, and reduce inflammation naturally.

I work closely with clients who have ongoing conditions like arthritis, fibromyalgia, or recurring back pain — offering a complementary approach alongside conventional treatment. Many find that reflexology reduces their reliance on pain medication over time.

  • Back, neck, and shoulder pain
  • Migraines and headaches
  • Joint stiffness and arthritis
  • Fibromyalgia
  • Plantar fasciitis and foot pain

Fertility & Reproductive Support

Fertility reflexology is one of my specialist areas. I work with clients who are trying to conceive naturally, going through IVF or IUI, or simply wanting to optimise their reproductive health. By focusing on the reflex points connected to the reproductive organs and endocrine system, we can help regulate hormones, improve blood flow, and reduce the stress that so often accompanies fertility challenges.

I understand how emotionally demanding this journey can be. My sessions provide a calm, supportive space where you can feel held and hopeful.

  • Natural conception support
  • IVF and IUI support
  • Hormonal imbalances and irregular cycles
  • PCOS and endometriosis
  • Pre-conception wellbeing

Sleep & Insomnia

Poor sleep affects every aspect of your life — energy, mood, concentration, immunity. Reflexology works with the nervous system and pineal gland reflexes to help regulate your sleep cycle naturally, without medication.

Clients frequently tell me that the night after a session is the best sleep they've had in weeks. Over time, regular treatments help establish healthier sleep patterns, making it easier to fall asleep, stay asleep, and wake feeling genuinely rested.

  • Difficulty falling asleep
  • Night waking and restlessness
  • Racing mind at bedtime
  • Fatigue and low energy
  • Shift work and disrupted routines

Digestive Health

The digestive system has extensive reflex points on the feet, making it particularly responsive to reflexology. I work with clients experiencing bloating, IBS, constipation, acid reflux, and general digestive discomfort — targeting the stomach, intestinal, and liver reflexes to encourage smoother, more comfortable digestion.

Because stress is a major contributor to digestive problems, the deep relaxation of reflexology provides a double benefit — calming the gut while calming the mind.

  • IBS symptoms
  • Bloating and discomfort
  • Constipation and sluggish digestion
  • Acid reflux
  • Stress-related gut issues

Maternity Reflexology

With specialist training in maternity reflexology, I support women throughout their pregnancy journey. Reflexology during pregnancy can help ease common discomforts like nausea, swollen ankles, back pain, and fatigue — while providing a deeply calming experience during a time of huge physical and emotional change.

In later pregnancy, reflexology can help prepare the body for labour. After birth, postnatal sessions support recovery, hormonal rebalancing, and the emotional adjustment to life with a new baby.

  • Morning sickness and nausea
  • Pregnancy-related back pain
  • Swollen feet and ankles
  • Labour preparation
  • Postnatal recovery
