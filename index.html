Hello, I'm Sophie

Helping you feel grounded, calm, and well

Reflexology is a beautifully gentle therapy that works with the reflex points on your feet to encourage your body's own healing response. Whether you're dealing with stress, aches and pains, hormonal changes, or simply need time to switch off, a session with me offers a deeply relaxing space to let go.

I work from The Wellbeing Rooms in the heart of Leamington Spa, where every treatment is tailored to you and your needs. I believe that looking after your feet is one of the kindest things you can do for your whole body.
